We Are Magazine. I went to the mall and collected about 10 catalogues, I then put in to all of the catalogues an insert that I had previously created saying "Do you really need any of these things?" and "Will any of this stuff make you happier?" I then put the catalogues back where I found them. My goal was to get at least one person to see these inserts and think twice about their consumption habits.
